Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nova Adventures?

Nova Adventures (also known as Micro Adventures) is a privacy-focused mobile tracker built to help you discover, log, and complete bite-sized daily quests and micro-milestones without public social feeds or tracking overhead.

How do I verify my account?

When signing up, an automated security email containing a transactional link is sent to your inbox. Simply tap that link from your mobile device to authorize and establish your data connection profile.

Where is my application data stored?

Your records and completions are securely transmitted using encrypted tunnels over HTTPS to our private, self-hosted backend infrastructure. We do not use third-party data analytics networks or public trackers, ensuring complete isolation of your adventure history.

Troubleshooting Connection & Login

Account Handshake Failures

If the application returns a timeout warning during the initial secure loading configuration, try the following steps:

  • Confirm that your mobile hardware is actively connected to cellular data or a functional local network.
  • Verify that standard outbound secure traffic (Port 443) isn't being forcefully dropped by your local proxy or active firewall.
  • Force clear your local application state or restart the sign-in sequence to renew the session certificates.

Data Control & Account Deletion

How do I request complete data erasure?

You retain absolute, sovereign control over your information footprint. If you decide to wipe your state completely from our servers, you can initiate a permanent purging pipeline by contacting our developer line directly. All stored records, hashed identifiers, and active database profiles will be immediately dropped from the host instance.
